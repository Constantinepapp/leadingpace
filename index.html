Features

Leading pace is a running metrics app.It calculates your running performance and can create a personalized running program depending on your fitness and current training

Fitness estimation

Calculate your running fitness and Vo2max using distance,duration,elevation and heart rate data

Training load

Watch your fatigue your fitness and your running form dynamicaly and plan your future workouts accordingly

Personalized Running Program

The app generates a personalized running program for you taking into account your aerobic fitness your training zones and training speeds and also your fatigue levels

Training Zones

Calculation of your heart rate training zones using the Karvonen formula and estimations on your training speed in each zone according to your aerobic fitness

Visual Analytics

Weekly and montly stress score and milage, Vo2max graphs and fitness-fatigue curves

Import from Strava

Link your strava account and sync your activities in seconds
