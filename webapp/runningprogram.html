How Running Program works

       Leading pace takes into account your VO2max estimation heart rate zones,running paces and current fatigue-fitness from the training load page.Using this data it calculates the target stress score you need to achieve in the next month in order your form to be consistent to form target value ( -10 by default ). The accuracy is higher for the first and second week and drops a little for the last two weeks. 

      The estimation of the second,third and forth week will only be accurate if you achive your goal in the last week. However small deviations ( <10% ) in your last weeks goal won't be a problem.

      As your VO2max and your running paces change the estimated paces and distances for future workouts will also be recalculated to your current aerobic fitness so it is normal your workout's pace and distance to change. Tss will stay the same

     This formula is based on fitness-fatigue curve which is based on scientific researches but it has it's flaws. So follow the program but listen to your body needs. It is adviced after every monthly program to have a "lighter" week to avoid injury before you start a new months program

     If you are a begiener in running and you are not used to run for 10 or more minutes straight it is adviced to run-walk on your first week. In order to stay in the correct zones run at a little higher speed than your goal to counterbalance your walking paces.

What form target to choose

If you are new to running, if you are recovering from an injury or finding your past generated program too hard for you can drop the form target value below 10

For new runners 7 is the greatest choise. You wil get the same aerobic and physical benefits of harder programs but you will transition slowly and give your body enough time to get used to running. In the next months consider increasing the form target slowly.

However for most people of all fitness capacities a form target of 10 is the default

Form target has nothing to do with aerobic capacity. An experienced runner and a new runner can both use the same form target but the app will generate different programs for each one.

Form target affects only the starting weekly stress score and mileage and your mileage increase in the next weeks.

Some of Leading pace's beginner programs will start you from a lower form target value in the first months

What is estimated distance

This is a heart rate base program not a speed one. Every indvidual exercise is calculated as target time in a specific heart rate zone. For display purposes the app calculates your speed in this zone and displays only the speed. The color of the activities name and speed is the color of the target heart rate zone for the exercise.

So every speed is calculated using your current aerobic fitness and distance using your current speed. As the program progresses and your aerobic speed increases the values on the table change and your speeds and estimated distance are recalculated.

This isn't a bug. This program is based on heart rate and stress score and those will stay the same. But as you get fitter your speeds to achieve a specific stress score increases so your estimated distance changes

Leading pace's running program is a dynamic one which changes as your body changes. This is the reason only a month of running program is generated each time. It is impossible to estimate your response to exercise running speeds and aerobic capacity in the next month so any estimation would be pointless
