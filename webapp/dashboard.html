Innaccurate Values and running program

If you have inaccurate Vo2max Values,Running index or running program check this area first before reporting the problem

Correct your Max and Rest heart rate.

Max and Rest heart rate is the base of leading pace's algorithm. If your Max and Rest values are inaccurate all values would be extremely off.Change them in the User Profile tab

Delete and re import activities after changing max and rest heart rate

Changing your max heart rate won't change your past activities data, it will affect only future activities, so after changing your heart rate delete and reimport your data

Train in lower/higher form target

If a running program is too fast/hard consider lowering form target for the first months.

If nothing worked

Contact me through the contact page. As the app is still in developement, the algorithm may have bugs or need calibrations and changes. Feel free to contact me
